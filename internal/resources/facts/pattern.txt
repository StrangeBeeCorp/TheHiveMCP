# Definition
A pattern is a MITRE ATT&CK technique or sub-technique entry loaded into TheHive from a catalog.
Patterns are the reference data used when creating procedures on cases and alerts.
Patterns are read-only — they are managed by administrators, not analysts.

# Usage
Before creating a procedure, search for the relevant pattern to obtain its patternId and confirm which tactics are valid for it.
Filter patterns by name, platform, or tactic to narrow results from the full catalog.
